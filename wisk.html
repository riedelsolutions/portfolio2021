Overview

As the Design Lead at WISK, I make the phrase “wearing many hats” as accurate as ever. My responsibilities have included a complete cross-platform product redesign and rebranding, feature design, asset creation, copywriting, and maintaining the product.

On top of my hands-on responsibilities, I manage the junior and mid-level designers, lead the marketing team in projects, and find the appropiate contractors for off-site development when our in-house devs are busy.

Redesign

When I started working at WISK, the platform direly needed a revamp — the incredibly powerful features were buried under a very outdated and confusing UI. As I redesigned it, I added new features that increased the value of WISK in the eyes of the users. Finally, I redesigned their branding completely, from its logo to its website and social media presence. Within 2 months after the new changes, the MRR increased 35%.

WISK Food

WISK Food expanded the company from beverage-only to food venues. This increased the potential reach three-fold. I was reponsible for developing key and major features, such as batch and recipe yields, median weights for inventory items, and price suggestion tool, with which food inventory would not work accurately (making competitors fail), as well as creating the interface and branding for it.

Mobile

WISK's most powerful tool, online and offline inventory, is possible thanks to the mobile app. I redesigned this as well to create a better user experience and features to be a more reliable, seamless product for both venue owners & employees. After my changes and optimization, the sign-ups increased ~25% and the conversion from free trials to paying users went up exponentially each month.
